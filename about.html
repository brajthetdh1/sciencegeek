About ScienceGeek

My journey began at one of the premier institutes in India, where I earned my M.Tech from IIT. The rigorous academic environment shaped my understanding and deepened my passion for science and mathematics.

After graduating, I spent several years as a software engineer, honing problem-solving skills and learning how to think logically and creatively to solve complex challenges.

Alongside my professional career, I discovered a love for teaching — especially guiding students preparing for competitive exams like JEE. Over the past 5 years, I’ve taught numerous batches, focusing on conceptual clarity, problem solving, and building confidence.

This passion inspired me to create ScienceGeek — a platform where learners can explore science and math topics in a friendly, approachable way, with engaging tutorials, quizzes, and exercises designed to make complex topics simple.

Whether you’re here to crack exams, enhance your understanding, or just satisfy your curiosity, I’m committed to helping you unlock your full potential.
